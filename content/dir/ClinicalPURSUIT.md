**Offering an enterprise solution for clinical trial management, this software excels in rapid study development and integrates advanced features such as intelligent electronic data capture (EDC) and clinical randomization technology, ensuring efficient and accurate data management.**

## Overview
The company specializes in offering solutions for clinical trial management, focused on enhancing the operational efficiencies of clinical trials. Their software is designed to streamline data collection and management processes, making it easier for researchers to conduct accurate and efficient clinical trials. The company stands out due to its innovative use of technology, which has become more critical, especially in the rapidly evolving landscape of clinical research, influenced significantly by challenges like the COVID-19 pandemic. With a strong market presence, they serve a diverse range of clients in the clinical research sector, helping them adapt to new trends and technologies.
## Products & Services 
The company provides advanced Electronic Data Capture (EDC) software and patient registry solutions. Their products are tailored to improve the speed and accuracy of clinical trials through intelligent electronic data capture systems, streamlined data management, and electronic patient-reported outcomes (ePRO). Additionally, they offer Clinical Randomization Technology (IRT) and Clinical Trial Supply Management (RTSM), alongside comprehensive solutions to meet the enterprise needs of clinical research organizations. Their demo platform, ClinicalPURSUIT, offers live demonstrations of their solutions' capabilities.
## Company Details 
The company is a significant player in the field of Clinical Trial Management Software (CTMS) and Electronic Data Capture (EDC) solutions, serving markets globally. With a focus on aiding the clinical research communities, they have tailored their services to address the evolving needs of clinical trials, coping with new challenges and leveraging technological advancements to deliver robust software solutions. The company has been operating for several years and continues to innovate to meet the demands of an ever-changing clinical trial environment.
